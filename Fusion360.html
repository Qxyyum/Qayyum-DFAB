This is my knight.
Step 1: I went to the internet to find a suitable knight sillhouette to begin with
Step 2: I outlined half the base and revolving it around its centre axis.
Step 3: I then traced out the knight silhouette and then extruding it making sure its was set as 'new body'.
Step 4: I then used shell to hollow out the base of the knight so when we print, we are sure not to use too much material.
Step 5: I added the details on the mane by sketching it on face of the knight and extruded it by selecting 'cut'
Step 6: I then array on path and selected path direction so it will rotate along the curve.
Step 7: Lastly, I made a new sketch on the face of the knight to make the eye cutout by seletcing cut.

These are the starirs instructed to be done in class. I initially had trouble but after trials and errors I was able to make the object. First I made a singular cube then I pattern it. I then selected 3 of the 4 cubes and pattern it until i had the base the desired shape. I then extruded each point making sure i marked new body if not the base shape will combine leaving me with an undesirable shape. Lastly I shelled the shape leaving me with 4mm thickness and a hollowed body.
